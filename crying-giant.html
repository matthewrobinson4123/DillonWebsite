Meet the sonic architects behind Crying Giant, Mitchell Dailey and Dillon Robinson. Their shared passion for sound, cultivated at Fanshawe College, blossomed into the creation of unique sound effects libraries. As self-proclaimed audio nerds, they revel in capturing the essence of the world through recording, creating, and molding peculiar sounds. At Crying Giant, expect a symphony of unconventional noises - because for them, the perfect sound for your next project might just be a recording away.
